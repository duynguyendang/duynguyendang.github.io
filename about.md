## Key Highlights & Projects

Throughout my career at **FPT Software** and **FPT Technology Solutions**, I have led several mission-critical initiatives that bridge complex data engineering with high-level solution architecture:

### Reporting System Migration (Jun 2024 – Present)
* **Role:** Lead Architect for a large-scale migration of a US retailer’s reporting infrastructure from Cognos to Power BI.
* **Scale:** Managing a 90+ member project team organized into 5 specialized sub-teams.
* **Governance:** Defined all technical standards, architectural patterns, and best practices to ensure consistency and quality across the entire project lifecycle.
* **Problem Solving:** Actively resolving complex technical and data-related bottlenecks to ensure seamless transition and data integrity.

### Asset Integrity through AI Platform (2020 – 2022)
* **Role:** Solution Architect & Offshore Technical Lead for an Oil & Gas company in the UAE.
* **Innovation:** Led the design and implementation of a cloud-agnostic data platform and built a Digital Twin to provide a comprehensive view of digital assets.
* **Optimization:** Leveraged Commercial-Off-the-Shelf (COTS) solutions and cutting-edge technologies to accelerate development while enhancing system functionality.

### Enterprise Data Platform (2020 – 2021)
* **Infrastructure:** Architected and built a secure, Azure-based data platform that successfully integrated data from over 150 disparate subsystems.
* **Technical Stack:** Implemented end-to-end data pipelines involving cleansing, transformation, and loading into **Azure Synapse DWH** using **Azure Data Factory** and **Databricks**.

* **Architecture:** Designed and implemented a microservices-based platform comprising over 30 independent services for real-time traffic analysis.
* **Performance:** Managed high-volume data processing handling 140 million messages per day from over 70,000 vehicles and 100,000 road segments.
